About mimicINT

Contact us

If you want to provide feedback or any other inquiry on mimicINTweb or the mimicINT workflow, please, contact Andreas Zanzoni via the following contact form by putting “mimicINT” in the message subject:

https://tagc.univ-amu.fr/fr/contact/mimicintweb

People involved in the mimicINT(web) development:

Sébastien A. Choteau, Kevin Maldonado, Aurélie Bergon, Marceau Cristianini, Mégane Boujeant, Lilian Drets, Christine Brun, Lionel Spinelli, Andreas Zanzoni.

Availability information

You can find a stand-alone version of the mimicINT workflow on GitHub:
https://github.com/TAGC-NetworkBiology/mimicINT

If you use mimicINT in your work, please cite our paper:
